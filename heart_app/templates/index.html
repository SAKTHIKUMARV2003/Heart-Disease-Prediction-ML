Understanding Heart Disease

Heart disease is a silent killer affecting millions across the globe. Early awareness, regular screening, and healthy lifestyle choices can significantly reduce the risk.

Common Causes
  • Poor diet rich in trans fats and sugar
  • Physical inactivity and sedentary habits
  • Smoking and excessive alcohol use
  • High blood pressure or cholesterol
  • Obesity and chronic stress
How to Prevent It
  • Follow a heart-healthy diet (low salt, low fat)
  • Exercise regularly
  • Quit smoking and limit alcohol
  • Monitor blood pressure & cholesterol
  • Practice stress management
Watch Out for Symptoms
  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue, dizziness, fainting
  • Palpitations or irregular heartbeat
  • Neck, jaw, throat, or back pain

Why Early Detection Matters

Many people show no symptoms until it's too late. HeartPredict is designed to support early detection by analyzing your health inputs with the help of a trained machine learning model.

This enables you to assess your risk levels and seek medical advice at the right time.
